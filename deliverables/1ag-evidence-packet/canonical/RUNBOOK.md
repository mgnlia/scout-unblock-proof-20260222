# 1Ag Canonical Evidence Runbook

## Objective
Provide deterministic trace capture and immutable citation pack for adversary validation.

## Steps
1. Execute `DETERMINISTIC_COMMANDS.md` in order.
2. Produce citations matching `SAMPLE_CITATION_PACK.md`.
3. Verify pass criteria in `ACCEPTANCE_CHECKLIST.md`.
4. Attach `UTC_TIMESTAMP.txt` and `SHA256SUMS.txt`.
5. Include `RAW_CAPTURE_REFS.md` as provenance index.
